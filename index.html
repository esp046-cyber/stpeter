Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is a St. Peter Memorial Plan?

A St. Peter Memorial Plan is a pre-need deathcare plan that guarantees complete funeral service, chapel, casket, and other memorial benefits at today's prices, payable in cash or easy installments (3, 5, or 10 years).

2. Can I avail a plan even if I'm already a senior citizen?

Yes. St. Peter accepts planholders up to 80 years old (Traditional Plans) and up to 75 years old (Chapel Deluxe Series). Some promo plans go up to 85 years old.

3. What is the difference between Traditional Plan and Life Plan?

Traditional Plan covers only the planholder. St. Peter Life Plan covers the planholder + up to 5 immediate family members (spouse, children, parents) in one contract.

4. Is the plan transferable?

Yes, 100% transferable to any person, any branch, nationwide, at no extra cost.

5. What happens if I miss a monthly payment?

30-day grace period. After grace period, the plan goes into lapse but can be reinstated within 2–3 years by paying arrears + small reinstatement fee.

6. Can I pay the plan in full (one-time payment) and get discount?

Yes. Cash/full payment within 30 days from approval entitles you to 5%–10% discount depending on the plan and current promo.

7. Are there hidden charges or additional fees upon death?

No. Everything is guaranteed: no additional payment even after 20–50 years, regardless of inflation.

8. Where can I use my St. Peter plan?

Any St. Peter Chapel, St. Peter Funeral Homes, and over 800+ affiliated funeral homes nationwide.

9. What if the planholder dies abroad?

St. Peter has International Assistance Program. Remains can be repatriated to the Philippines and all benefits still apply.

10. How can I check my balance or statement of account?

Call 02-8371-0888 / 02-8371-1888 or visit any St. Peter branch nationwide. You may also text SOA to 0917-537-9837.

11. Is St. Peter financially stable?

Yes. St. Peter Life Plan, Inc. is the largest and most trusted deathcare company in the Philippines with over ₱60 billion trust fund (as of 2025) and regulated by the Insurance Commission.

12. How do I file a claim when the planholder passes away?

Call St. Peter Hotline 02-8371-1888 or go to the nearest branch with death certificate and valid IDs. Service is provided within 24–48 hours.

13. What are the benefits included in my St. Peter Life Plan?

Your family is entitled to complete funeral services, including embalming, casket, chapel use, hearse, and memorial service package chosen during application, guaranteed by St. Peter Chapels nationwide.

14. Who can claim the benefits from my St. Peter Life Plan?

The legal heirs or authorized representatives of the planholder, with proper documentation such as death certificate and valid IDs.

15. What are the requirements to claim the benefits?

Original Life Plan Agreement (LPA) – Planholder’s copy, Original Certificate of Full Payment (if fully paid), Registered Death Certificate with seal from Local Civil Registrar or PSA, and valid IDs of claimant.

16. How can my family claim the insurance benefits?

Visit any St. Peter Life Plan branch with required documents. For insurable planholders, submit for death claim processing; benefits are released upon approval.

17. What is the St. Peter Chapels E-BUROL?

The FREE online viewing facility for wakes, allowing families abroad or far away to join virtually via internet, bridging distances in support of public service.

18. Can I terminate my plan and get money back?

Yes, subject to termination value schedule. Visit any branch Mon-Sat 8am-5pm with Certificate of Full Payment for money-back guarantee on eligible plans.

19. What is the age requirement for availing a plan with insurance coverage?

From 18 to 55 years old for accident insurance; 18 to 59 years old for life insurance coverage upon availing the plan.

20. Is St. Peter regulated and trustworthy?

Yes, regulated by the Insurance Commission. Trust funds are held by top banks like BPI, BDO, Metrobank for security. Established since 1970 as DeathCare Experts.

21. What payment methods are accepted for St. Peter plans?

Cash, check, credit card (Visa/Mastercard), bank transfer, or auto-debit from BPI, BDO, Metrobank accounts. Installment options: 36/60/120 months.

22. Does the plan include lot or mausoleum space?

Yes, optional add-ons: niche, lot, or mausoleum from St. Peter Memorial Parks, payable separately or bundled in premium plans.

23. What if the beneficiary is a minor?

Legal guardian must present guardianship papers or court order. St. Peter assists in processing claims for minors under 18.

24. Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, upgrade to higher package anytime by paying difference in contract value, subject to medical underwriting if over age limit.

25. What is the processing time for plan application?

Approval within 3-5 working days after submission of complete documents and initial payment. Faster for cash payments.

26. Are there plans for OFWs?

Yes, special OFW packages with flexible remittances via Western Union, BDO Kabayan, or online banking. International hotline support available.

27. Does St. Peter offer group or corporate plans?

Yes, customized group plans for companies, associations, or LGUs, with bulk discounts and employer-sponsored payments.

28. What happens if the planholder has pre-existing conditions?

No issue for basic plans. For insurance add-ons, disclosure required; may need medical exam if over 50 or high-risk.

29. Can I pay via GCash or Maya?

Yes, e-wallet payments accepted at branches or online portal. Scan QR code for instant confirmation.

30. How do I contact St. Peter customer service?

31. What is the difference between St. Peter Traditional Life Plan and Cremation Life Plan?

Traditional includes casket and burial setup; Cremation covers first-class cremation services from St. Peter facilities, suited for modern, simplified memorials.

32. Can I assign the plan to a deceased person?

Yes, assign to any deceased person if balance is paid before service; insurance coverage terminates, but family receives cash assistance via life/accident insurance.

33. Is the plan service ever terminated before use?

No, the plan service is never terminated unless the service is used or fully terminated by the planholder.

36. Are St. Peter plans anti-inflationary?

Yes, pre-paid plans guarantee your chosen memorial service package at today's prices, protecting against future inflation.
